Overview

Purpose

Calling all thrill-seekers and nature-lovers! If you are looking for an exhilarating and unforgettable experience on the water, you have comes to the right place. We aim to provide adventurers of all levels of experience with a thrilling, but safe experience! Whether you are interested in a scenic float, or a much more challenging rapids experience, we have packages to suit your needs.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Anyone wanting to experience the beautiful and/or challenging aspects of nature through a unique rafting experience. We also offer a variety of packages and price points for those more budget-minded adventures. What do they want? A thrilling adventure outside of the typical vacation experiences. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? Pricing for packages, times of the year when packages are available, reviews about the company, and pictures of what the adventures might look like. What are their needs that aren't being met? White Water Rafting is hardly accessible to beginners or those who can't afford the equipment outright, we provide a low-barrier of entry into this thrilling hobby. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? Our site is Desktop, laptop, and mobile friendly.
